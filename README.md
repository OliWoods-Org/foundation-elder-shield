> **Americans over 60 lost $4.9 billion to financial fraud in 2024.** AI-generated deepfake calls impersonate grandchildren, banks, and government agencies. Only 1 in 44 cases reaches authorities. Projected $40B in AI-driven fraud by 2027.

## The Solution

Monitor transactions for anomalies. Detect deepfake voices in real-time. Alert designated family guardians. Verify suspicious calls. Auto-report to Adult Protective Services.

## Agents

**TransactionMonitor** — Flag sudden large withdrawals, new payees, gift card purchases
**DeepfakeDetector** — Real-time voice/video deepfake detection during phone calls
**GuardianAlert** — Plain-language alerts to designated family members
**ScamVerifier** — 'Did Social Security really call you?' verification chatbot
**ReportAssistant** — Auto-file reports to Adult Protective Services

## Safety

MANDATORY: If financial exploitation is confirmed, alert local APS and law enforcement immediately.

## Data Sources

FBI IC3 elder fraud reports, FTC Consumer Sentinel, CFPB complaints, open banking APIs, ASVspoof deepfake datasets
